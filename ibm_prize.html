Ontology Alignment Evaluation Initiative

IBM Research Sponsors the Instance Matching tracks

IBM Research sponsors a prize of $750 for the most complete and competitive tool across the OAEI 2017 instance matching tracks: Process Model Matching, Instance Matching, and HOBBIT Link Discovery. In addition, as an incentive for participation, there will also be a prize of $250 for a system producing good results in the novel HOBBIT Link Discovery track.

Depending on the number of participants and results the prizes may be split into 2 or more participants.

Winner of the Prize!

AML is the winner of the 2017 IBM Research prize. Congratulations!

We take this opportunity to thanks and acknowledge the other participants of the instance mathcing related tracks: I-Match, Legato, LogMap, njuLink, RADON and Silk.
